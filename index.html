About

Researcher, educator, and applied AI practitioner.

My work focuses on the societal impacts and economics of AI, digital communication, user-generated content, and healthcare information systems. I work across machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and econometrics to answer consequential real-world questions.

Areas of Expertise

  • Natural language processing, LLMs, and generative AI

  • Healthcare information systems
