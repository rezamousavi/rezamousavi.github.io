About

Researcher, educator, and applied AI practitioner.

My work focuses on how AI operates, how people consume and respond to AI-generated content, and how those interactions influence individual behavior and society at large. In parallel with my research, I develop AI-based applications, including luukai.io, an AI engine optimization platform for small businesses, and peerpen.app, an AI tool for academic peer review.

At UVA, I teach undergraduate and graduate students about the inner workings of AI and how it can be used to address practical and consequential problems.
