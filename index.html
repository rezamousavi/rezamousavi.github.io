About

Researcher, educator, and applied AI practitioner.

My work focuses on the societal impacts and economics of AI, digital communication, user-generated content, and healthcare information systems. I work across machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and econometrics to study consequential real-world questions.

I teach AI and business analytics, research methods, computer programming, and project management at the undergraduate, masters, and doctoral levels. Before academia, I worked as Lead Data Scientist at State Farm Insurance.
